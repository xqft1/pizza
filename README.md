# Pizza

**Turn SATO into PIZZA.**

PIZZA is a fully on-chain experiment that combines Bitcoin-inspired scarcity with a pizza-powered bonding curve.

Users contribute **SATO** through the **Pizza Oven** and receive freshly baked **PIZZA** according to a transparent mathematical issuance curve.

# What Is PIZZA?

PIZZA is an ERC-20 token deployed on Ethereum with a maximum supply of:

**21,000,000 PIZZA**

Just like Bitcoin's 21 million supply, PIZZA can never exceed its hard cap.

Instead of traditional mining, PIZZA is baked.

Users send SATO into the Pizza Oven and receive PIZZA according to an on-chain issuance curve designed to gradually reduce emissions as supply grows.

---

# How Baking Works

1. Acquire SATO
2. Visit the Pizza Oven
3. Bake SATO into PIZZA
4. Enjoy your freshly baked tokens

The Pizza Oven is the only contract capable of minting new PIZZA.

As more SATO is contributed, the curve naturally reduces the amount of PIZZA issued per unit of SATO.

Early bakers receive more pizza.

Late bakers receive less pizza.

The oven never sleeps.

# Decentralization

PIZZA was designed to minimise trust requirements.

### Token Contract

✅ Deployer mint rights revoked

✅ DEFAULT_ADMIN_ROLE renounced

✅ Hard cap enforced on-chain

✅ No ability to create new minters

✅ No hidden mint functions

### Minting

The Pizza Oven contract is the sole authorised minter.

All new PIZZA enters circulation through the public baking process defined by the protocol.
